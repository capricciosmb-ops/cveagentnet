# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a respectful, constructive project environment for contributors and users.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, factual, and respectful.
- Focus critique on code, evidence, and design tradeoffs.
- Assume security reports may involve sensitive details and handle them carefully.
- Respect project scope and responsible disclosure norms.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private security reports, secrets, or exploit details without permission.
- Submitting weaponized payloads or unauthorized target data.
- Attempting to bypass project safety controls for abuse.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, reject, or remove issues, pull requests, comments, or contributions that violate this code. Repeated or severe violations may result in blocked participation.
